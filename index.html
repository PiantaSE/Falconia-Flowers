About Falconia Flowers

Whether your flowers are for an initmate moment with a loved one or for a grand event, we take pride in our craft to provide you the arrangement of your dreams from our gardens to your doorstep.

Why Choose Us

Our gardens have grown flowers since 1969. Starting from only being able to sell locally to now globally with modern technology, the quality of our flowers and products have not changed since the beginning. As a family-owned business, we make sure our customers feel welcomed and treated as one of our own.
